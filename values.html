Courage

Courage is an extremely important trait to have. When tryinh new things courage can help you overcome any insecurities and allow you to perservere even if you aren't 100% sure what you are doing.

Persistence

Persistence allows us to keep going even when we are uncertain or stuck.

Respect

Respect is an integral part of working with others. If you dont respect those people and their ideas then you cannot work effectivley as a team.

Teamwork

Trust

Trust is extremely important to build community and allow teams to work well together.
